⚡ Founder & Builder

Josh
Virostek

I didn't build EnergyOS because I had a great idea in a boardroom. I built it because I watched myself — and everyone around me — collapse under the pressure of college life with zero tools to actually manage it.

Junior year nearly broke me. I was pulling all-nighters, running on caffeine and anxiety, and checking every productivity app imaginable. None of them could tell me why I felt like garbage at 2pm or why my best study sessions happened randomly at 11pm.

Then I started digging into the research on heart rate variability, circadian rhythms, and sleep debt. I started logging my Apple Watch data and noticed patterns my calendar app would never catch. I built a crude spreadsheet model to predict my "peak windows" — and my grades jumped.

That spreadsheet became EnergyOS. A real-time, AI-powered operating system for your daily energy — not your schedule.

I'm a college student, not a VC-backed executive. I'm building this for us — the students who are trying their hardest and still feel like they're losing.
