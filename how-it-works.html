The System

How EnergyOS
Actually Works

From raw biometric data to a fully optimized daily routine — here's the engine under the hood.

⌚
Step 01

Apple Watch Syncs Continuously

EnergyOS connects to Apple Health and reads heart rate, HRV, sleep stages, activity rings, and SpO2 throughout the day — passively, no manual input required.

🧠
Step 02

AI Builds Your Energy Profile

Over your first 7 days, the AI learns your personal baseline — when your HRV peaks, how your sleep quality affects afternoon focus, and what caffeine patterns work for your body specifically.

📊
Step 03

Daily Energy Score Generated

Each morning you receive a 0–100 Energy Score — a composite of sleep quality, HRV trend, sleep debt, and caffeine timing — so you know exactly what kind of day you're walking into.

🌀
Step 04

Distraction Spirals Detected in Real Time

The AI monitors behavioral patterns and HRV drops to identify when you've entered a distraction loop — doomscrolling, app-switching, zoning out — and sends a gentle but firm reset nudge before the hour is gone.

💡
Step 05

Smart Resets Suggested

Instead of just alerting you, EnergyOS suggests the right reset for that moment: a 10-min walk to spike BDNF, a 20-min nap during your circadian dip, or a hydration + stretch break to restore focus.

🔥
Step 06

You Perform at Your Peak

Over time, your performance baseline rises. Better sleep hygiene, better caffeine timing, fewer wasted hours — EnergyOS doesn't just manage today's energy, it trains you to have more of it every week.

understands biology

EnergyOS isn't just a notification app. It's a multi-layer machine learning system trained on sleep science, HRV research, and chronobiology — applied to your personal data in real time.

The model uses a rolling 14-day window of your biometrics to continuously recalibrate your personal baselines. What's "low HRV" for one person might be normal for another — EnergyOS knows the difference.
