FAQ

Common Questions

Quick answers before you reach out.

When will EnergyOS launch?

We're targeting a beta launch later this year for waitlist members. The exact date depends on development progress. Join the waitlist to get first access and updates directly from Josh.

Do I need an Apple Watch?

Yes — Apple Watch is required for the full EnergyOS experience since the core features rely on real-time HRV, heart rate, and sleep data from Apple Health. Support for other wearables may come later.

Is my health data private?

Absolutely. EnergyOS processes your biometric data on-device whenever possible. We will never sell your data or share it with third parties. Privacy is a core design principle, not an afterthought.

Will EnergyOS be free?

Early waitlist members will get extended free access. The final pricing model is still being finalized, but Josh is committed to keeping EnergyOS affordable for students.

Can I invest in or partner with EnergyOS?

Josh is open to conversations with aligned investors and partners. Use the contact form above and select "Investor Inquiry" or "Partnership / Collab" — he'll get back to you directly.

I'm a journalist / content creator. Can I cover EnergyOS?

Yes! Josh loves talking about the science behind EnergyOS and the student burnout epidemic. Reach out via the form or email josh@energyos.app with your publication/channel details.
